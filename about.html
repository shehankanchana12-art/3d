Who We Are

Bridging Enterprise Tech
With Local Business

Soft Vision Technologies (Pvt) Ltd — operating as SoftVision IT Group — has spent over fifteen years evolving from a targeted vendor of localized business management systems into a diversified enterprise platform handler and BPO provider.

Our decision to open a regional branch at the Mahiyangana Commercial Complex (MCC) in Mahiyanganaya signals a strategic commitment to decentralize high-tier digital infrastructure beyond the Western Province — bringing it directly to the rural economic drivers of Sri Lanka.

2008 – 2010
Foundation
Soft Vision Technologies founded in Colombo. Initial focus on localized business management systems and ERP deployments for Sri Lankan enterprises.
2012 – 2015
Alliance Formation
Formation of the tripartite alliance: Soft Vision Technologies + Busy InfoTech (ERP/accounting) + Skyb Overseas Consultants (career routing). Surpassed 300 active clients.
2017 – 2020
Cloud & Scale
Launched cloud-native product suite. Deployed Docker containerization, PostgreSQL + Redis stack. 750+ clients across international borders. 99.99% SLA achieved.
2023 – 2024
Industrial Specialization
Launched Fuel Billing Systems and Container Yard Management with 3D spatial mapping. Integration Platform hit 200+ pre-engineered connectors.
2025 – Present
MCC Mahiyanganaya
Opened the MCC regional branch — bringing enterprise software directly to Uva and Eastern province businesses for the first time. Local support. Local language. Local impact.
